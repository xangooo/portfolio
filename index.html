Undergraduate Thesis: Effects of Thixotropy on Remolded Clay Soil

Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)

2021 ‚Äì 2022 | Dhaka, Bangladesh

  • Investigated the time-dependent recovery of shear strength (**thixotropy**) in remolded clay soil samples.
  • Found that the rate of shear strength improvement diminishes as water content increases, indicating dependence on soil activity.
  • **Key Finding:** Underscored the necessity of incorporating curing time provisions in strength examination procedures for remolded clay.
